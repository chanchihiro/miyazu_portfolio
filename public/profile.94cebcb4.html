MIYAZU DAIZO

MIYAZU Daizo is professor in the Faculty of Culture and Sport Policy at ToinUniversity of Yokohama. He started his teaching career as the homeroomteacher of a special class in a public elementary school and continued in officein national elementary schools for 27 years. From there, he was transferred tothe position of associate professor at Toin University of Yokohama. He hasauthored and edited elementary school Japanese language textbooks,education books and children’s books, including his first novel, Zuka-Men! MaleStaff in Takarazuka Revue Company, an all-female musical theatre troup(Kosaido Publishing) in 2014.
